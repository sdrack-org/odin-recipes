Lasagna

Lasagna is a wide, flat sheet of pasta. Lasagna can refer to either the type of noodle or to the typical lasagna dish which is a dish made with several layers of lasagna sheets with sauce and other ingredients, such as meats and cheese, in between the lasagna noodles.

Ingredients

  • noodles
  • olive oil
  • ground beef
  • onion
  • salt
  • pepper
  • can of tomatoes
  • ricotta
  • eggs
  • chopped parsely

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Boil Water, add salt
  3. Heat olive oil. Add ground beef until brown. Drain Fat and add tomatoes. Season with Salt and pepper to taste
  4. combine ricotta and eggs, set aside.
  5. Layer noodles, tomatoe mixture ,ricotta mixture. Repeat for each layer.
  6. Bake for 35-45 minutes.
  7. Let cool 15 minutes. Garnish with parsely. Serve warm
