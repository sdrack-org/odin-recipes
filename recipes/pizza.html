Pizza

This Quick and Easy Pizza Dough requires just 5 minutes of prep! Top with your favorite toppings, bake and you can have homemade pizza in 30 minutes!

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Pepperonni
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Bacon
  • Extra Topping

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven
  2. Roll out dough to pizza shape
  3. Spread tomato sauce around pizza dough,evenly
  4. Add cheese,Pepperonni, Bacon and any additional toppings
  5. Bake for 35-45 minutes.
  6. Let cool 10 minutes. Serve warm
